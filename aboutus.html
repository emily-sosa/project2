ABOUT US

We believe that all people who live in the U.S. should have the opportunity to achieve their full potential. Over the years, we’ve been at the forefront of many of the country’s greatest challenges when it comes to immigration issues, and we play a major leadership role in addressing the real-life impact of policies that affect the ability of low-income immigrants to prosper and thrive.

Access to Public Benefits: advocating for policies that allow immigrants to access public benefits, such as healthcare and education. These policies can have economic implications by ensuring that immigrants can stay healthy and pursue educational opportunities that may lead to higher-paying jobs.

Economic Contributions: Immigrants often make substantial economic contributions to the United States. They start businesses, pay taxes, and help stimulate economic growth in communities. We want to ensure that immigrants have opportunities to contribute to the economy.
